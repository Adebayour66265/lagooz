What Our client Says About Us

Lagos Academy is a tutorial institution teaching those preparing for GCE, SSCE, UME, NECO, TOEFL, GMAT and other related local and foreign examinations. The performance of our students has always been creditable and commendable. This is because the teaching is handled by seasoned and experienced graduate teachers and lecturers.

The Computer College was established in January 2005 in consonance with global I.T. requirements. The world suddenly became a computer village which enhanced international communication. So, in our effort to keep pace with the global change, the computer college was born. Since its inception in 2005, Lagos Academy Computers has churned out more than 1,000 graduates who are now occupying key positions in different corporate organisations and the civil service.

LAGOOZ SCHOOLS

Lagos Academy, a precursor to Lagooz Schools, was established in January 2002 to assuage the yearnings of innumerable Lagosians, young and old, who were clamouring for education but could not affort the wherewithal and time to go into full regular schools. While this search was going on, a man with outstanding educational pedigree, Otunba Yomi Otubela, felt touched as a wave of ideas on how best to cater for the educational needs of humanity, especially in Lagos State then, was passing through his mind and giving him sleepless nights. Then a burning zeal to take the bull by the horns (I mean, the urge to take a leap) metamorphosed into what today has trained many enterprising graduates in the Nigerian Society.
